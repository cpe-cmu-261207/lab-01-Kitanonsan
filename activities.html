Oscilloscope Oscilloscope is a device that allows us to see graphically the electrical signal. This is my high school project. My friends and I built an Oscilloscope with Arduino Uno ,TFT Touchscreen and Adafruit Analog Acceleromete.

The Truth The Truth is a freeware role-playing horror video game. The game features puzzle and RPG elements, and revolves around a girl who is trapped in a haunted mansion, and is stalked by a ghost. This game was developed by using Scractch programming language.

RUN:AI RUN:AI is a casual endless runner game. This game was my first time using Unreal Engine 5. A gameplay is easy to understand. Just collect coins and avoid crashing obstacles. I wish this will be a fun game to play when you break during the day.
